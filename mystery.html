Mystery Class


Little is known about the Mystery Class Dragons, due to their stealthy and sneaky nature. Dragons in this class are generally more feared than those in other classes. This Class tends to include the most diverse and interesting range of dragons out of all, most of which exibit extremely rare and unique traits.
